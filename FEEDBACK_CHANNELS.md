- `priority: low` - Low priority

## Surveys

We conduct regular surveys to gather structured feedback:

### Weekly Quick Survey

- **Frequency**: Every Friday
- **Duration**: 2-3 minutes
- **Topics**: Recent changes, quick feedback
- **Link**: Sent via email and Discord

### Monthly Detailed Survey

- **Frequency**: Last week of each month
- **Duration**: 10-15 minutes
- **Topics**: Feature usage, satisfaction, priorities
- **Link**: Sent via email and Discord

### End-of-Alpha Survey

- **Timing**: End of alpha testing period
- **Duration**: 20-30 minutes
- **Topics**: Comprehensive feedback, future direction
- **Link**: Sent via email and Discord

## Feedback Impact

### How We Use Your Feedback

1. **Triage**: All feedback is reviewed and categorized
2. **Prioritize**: Issues are prioritized based on impact
3. **Plan**: High-priority items added to roadmap
4. **Implement**: Features and fixes are developed
5. **Release**: Changes are released in updates
6. **Follow-up**: We notify you when addressed

### Feedback Metrics

We track:
- Response time
- Resolution time
- User satisfaction
- Feature adoption
- Bug recurrence

### Transparency

- Public roadmap on GitHub Projects
- Monthly progress reports
- Release notes with attribution
- Community voting on features
